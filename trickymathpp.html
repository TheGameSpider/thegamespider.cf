Definitions

"TrickyMath" or "The App" is the android application "TrickyMath" (cf.thegamespider.trickymath), downloaded from Google Play or any other source.
"We", "Us" or "Our" refers to the NumBlend team - Author of the App.
"User", "You", "Your" or "Player" is anything and/or anyone who uses TrickyMath
"The Information" is any information you provide to Us


Privacy Policy

What information do we store:

  • Your username
  • Your IP address
  • Your Identification Code
  • Your game progress
  • Data about your device: Device Model, Device OS, Device Language

How do you provide us this information:

Every User have to choose their username on the first start of The App. This username is stored to Our database. All the other information listed above is stored automatically.


Who can see this information:

Your username and Your game progress can be seen by any Player and Us.
Your Identification Code can be seen by you and Us. Do NOT share this code with anyone. You can provide this code to Us, if you want to restore your lost game progress.
Any other information listed above can be seen by Us. You can request from Us all the information We have stored about you.


How we use this information:

We use your IP address to determine your approximate location.
Your Device Information helps us to develop the application.
We do NOT share any of your information with anyone NOT listed above


Terms of Use

Username Policy

Every User have to choose their username on the first start of The App. The username has to comply with these rules:

  • Must be at least 3 characters long
  • Cannot be obscene or profane
  • Cannot contain an email address or web address (URL)
  • Cannot contain a phone number
  • Cannot contain your full name or any other personal information if you are under 13
All accounts that do not comply with these rules will be removed.

Server Usage Rules

By connecting to the TrickyMath server you agree with shese rules:

  • It is phorbitten to spam requests to intentionally slow down the server or disturb other players.
